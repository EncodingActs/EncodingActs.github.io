Introduction:
The Martial Arts Ontology (MAont) describes martial arts as an entity of embodied knowledge with a conceptual space incorporating three dimensions: the kinesthetic, stylistic, and social dimensions. The MAont Version 1.0, extended from its basis, accentuates the role of people in transmitting and (re)developing different styles and expands the Master module accordingly.

MAont is available at the url, licensed under the terms of the MIT license.
